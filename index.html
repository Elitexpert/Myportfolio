About Me
Background

I’m Alter Iterskiy, a civil/construction engineering student at NYC College of Technology with hands-on experience as a special inspector on New York City construction projects. I enjoy working at the intersection of structural design, field inspections, and practical problem solving.

Outside of school and work, I love to create my very own conceptual ideas. I also like to tinker alot thus i love spending time with my father in his construction business learning the trade of construction and civil engineering.
